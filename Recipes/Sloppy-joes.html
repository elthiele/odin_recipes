Sloppy Joes

What is it?

An old-time classic meal

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 hamburger buns
